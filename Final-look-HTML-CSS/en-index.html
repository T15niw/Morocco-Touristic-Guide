Why Morocco?

Morocco is a country where African, Arab and European cultures are entwined.
Located at the mouth of the Mediterranean Sea, Morocco is the closest point of contact to Europe for Africa as a continent.
Morocco is a land of tolerance, a blend of cultures and religions, a country known for its amazing hospitality.
The fascinating mixture of history, art, food, music and architecture makes Morocco an unforgettable destination.
